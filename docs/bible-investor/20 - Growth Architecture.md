## 6. Paid Acquisition (Secondary Growth Channel)

### 6.1 When to Use Paid Ads

**Paid acquisition makes sense when**:
- Organic growth hits a plateau
- LTV is high enough to sustain paid CAC
- You want to accelerate growth (not just achieve it)

**NGN's stance**: Primarily organic, supplemental paid

**When to activate paid** (2026-2027):
- Once organic growth reaches 5K artists (critical mass)
- Once artist LTV > $3,000 (can support $100+ CAC)
- Once conversion funnel is optimized (3%+ conversion)

### 6.2 Paid Channels

**Channel 1: Music-specific platforms** (Bandcamp, SoundCloud)
- Target: Independent musicians
- Cost: $0.50-2.00 per click
- Conversion: 2-5%

**Channel 2: Social media** (Instagram, TikTok)
- Target: Music creators, young artists
- Cost: $0.10-0.50 per click
- Conversion: 1-3%

**Channel 3: Podcast sponsorships** (music industry podcasts)
- Target: Serious musicians
- Cost: $500-5,000 per episode
- Conversion: Higher (word-of-mouth effect)

**Channel 4: Music festivals/conferences** (booth, sponsorship)
- Target: Touring musicians, label execs
- Cost: $10K-50K per event
- Conversion: Very high (direct conversation)

**Recommendation**: Focus on Channel 3-4 (podcasts + events) for highest ROI

### 6.3 Paid Budget Recommendation

**2025**: $100K (test channels)
**2026**: $250K (scale what works)
**2027**: $500K (accelerate growth late in year)

## 8. Retention: Keeping Artists Happy

### 8.1 Why Retention Matters

**Growth equation**: Ending Users = Starting Users + New Users âˆ’ Churned Users

**Example**:
- Start: 2,847 artists
- Add: 2,153 new artists
- Churn: 500 artists (if churn is 10%)
- End: 4,500 artists (short of 5,000 target)

**Churn destroys growth.** Must maintain >95% annual retention.

### 8.2 Churn Prevention

**Reason #1: Not earning money**
- **Prevention**: Dashboard shows earnings immediately, Sparks are real money
- **Outcome**: Artists see value quickly (payback period: 1-2 weeks)

**Reason #2: No fans discovering**
- **Prevention**: Radio partnerships + charts guarantee visibility
- **Outcome**: Artists get discovered (even if slowly)

**Reason #3: Platform is buggy/crashes**
- **Prevention**: 99.9% uptime SLA, fast response times
- **Outcome**: Platform feels reliable

**Reason #4: Community feels dead**
- **Prevention**: Social engagement, Sparks, artist responses
- **Outcome**: Community feels alive

**Result**: Churn should be <5% annually (very low)

### 8.3 Retention Metrics

**Track these every month**:
- **Active artists** (posted content in last 30 days): Target >85% of onboarded
- **Earning artists** (earned money in last month): Target >60%
- **Engaged artists** (Sparked or commented in last month): Target >40%
- **Churn rate** (artists who become inactive): Target <1%/month

**If churn > 2%/month, something is broken. Diagnose and fix.**
