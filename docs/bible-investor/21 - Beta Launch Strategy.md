### 1.2 What Worked (Validate in Beta)

#### Success 1: Radio Partnerships

**Expected**: Would take 12 months to sign 20 stations
**Actual**: Signed 47 stations in 2 months
**Why**: Free platform + genuine value (SMR data insights)
**Lesson**: Radio stations are hungry for modernization

**Action for Series A**: Hire dedicated radio partnerships team to scale to 150+ stations

#### Success 2: Artist Word-of-Mouth

**Expected**: Would need $100K marketing spend to reach artists
**Actual**: 60%+ of artists joined via referral or word-of-mouth
**Why**: Artists who make money tell other artists
**Lesson**: Product-led growth works if product delivers

**Action for Series A**: Double down on artist success stories (case studies, testimonials)

#### Success 3: Zero Payment Disputes

**Expected**: 5-10% of royalties would be disputed
**Actual**: 0 disputes
**Why**: Rights ledger forces clarity upfront
**Lesson**: Transparency prevents fraud

**Action for Series A**: Use this as competitive advantage (messaging: "Only platform with zero payment disputes")

#### Success 4: Spark Tipping Demand

**Expected**: Would need incentives to get fans to tip
**Actual**: Fans naturally Spark artists ($XXX,XXX in voluntary tips)
**Why**: Direct connection between fan and artist
**Lesson**: Tipping model is viable (proven by Twitch, Patreon, Substack)

**Action for Series A**: Invest in Spark UX/gamification to drive more tipping

### 1.3 What Didn't Work (Learn & Fix)

#### Challenge 1: SMR Ingestion Is Still Semi-Manual

**Problem**: Radio stations submit CSV, system parses, humans resolve ambiguous artist names
**Impact**: Limits scale (can handle 47 stations, but 150+ would require more humans)

**Solution in 2026**: AI-powered artist matching (reduce manual resolution from 30% to 5%)

**Cost**: $100K engineering effort
**Timeline**: Q2 2026

#### Challenge 2: Mobile Experience Is Poor

**Problem**: Artists access dashboard on mobile, experience is clunky
**Impact**: Artists spend less time engaging

**Solution in 2026**: Native iOS app (or high-quality PWA)

**Cost**: $200K development
**Timeline**: Q3 2026

#### Challenge 3: Low Awareness Outside Music Industry

**Problem**: Target audience (independent musicians) don't know NGN exists
**Impact**: Word-of-mouth alone won't reach all 10K target artists

**Solution in 2026**: Content marketing + podcast sponsorships + industry events

**Cost**: $500K/year
**Timeline**: Ongoing

**Risk**: Engineering delays on iOS app / features

**Mitigation**:
- Experienced engineering team (not first-time founders)
- Agile methodology (ship fast, iterate)
- Outsource non-core (leverage contractors if needed)

**Probability**: Low (team has shipped multiple products)

### 7.2 Market Risk

**Risk**: Artists don't adopt NGN as fast as projected

**Mitigation**:
- Multiple growth channels (radio + organic + paid)
- Low CAC ($50-100 via radio referrals)
- High LTV ($1,500+ per artist)
- Churn proof (85% retention already at low scale)

**Probability**: Low (traction already proven in beta)

- By 2027, NGN has 10K artists + radio partnerships
