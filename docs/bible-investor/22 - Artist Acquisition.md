# Chapter 22: Artist Acquisition Roadmap

## Executive Summary

NGN's path to 10,000 artists by 2027 relies on **four diversified acquisition channels**: (1) **Radio station referrals** (lowest CAC: $50-75, highest volume), (2) **Aggregator partnerships** (DistroKid, TuneCore, CD Baby reach 5M+ artists), (3) **Organic/word-of-mouth** (60% of current growth, self-sustaining), (4) **Paid marketing** (podcasts, events, social, lowest priority but accelerator). Each channel has different economics; combined, they create redundancy and resilience. Target: 2,153 new artists per year (2025-2027) at blended CAC of $75-100 per artist, delivering LTV/CAC ratio of 15-20x (exceptional for growth-stage company).

## 1. Business Context

### 1.1 Artist Acquisition Funnel

**How artists find NGN**:

```
Awareness → Interest → Consideration → Conversion → Retention

Awareness:
├─ Radio station says "Artists on our charts earn money on NGN"
├─ Friend artist says "I made $500 on NGN this month"
├─ Podcast mentions NGN
├─ Google search finds NGN article
└─ Social media sees artist using NGN

Interest:
├─ Artist checks NGN website
├─ Artist reads case study ("How Artist X Made $5K")
├─ Artist watches demo video
└─ Artist talks to other artists using NGN

Consideration:
├─ Artist compares to Spotify/Bandcamp/Audius
├─ Artist checks artist testimonials
├─ Artist sees earnings breakdown
└─ Artist verifies radio partnerships are real

Conversion:
├─ Artist creates account (5 min)
├─ Artist uploads first song (15 min)
├─ Artist sees dashboard (wow moment: "I can earn here")
└─ Artist posts content (commitment)

Retention:
├─ Artist earns money (Sparks flow in)
├─ Artist sees other artists' success
├─ Artist builds fan community
└─ Artist becomes platform evangelist
```

**Key insight**: First week is critical. If artist earns money in week 1, retention is very high (>95%). If no earnings by week 1, churn is high (40%+).

## 2. Channel 1: Radio Station Referrals (Biggest Opportunity)

### 2.1 How It Works

**Radio station integration creates natural referral loop**:

```
Step 1: Station integrates NGN SMR data
Step 2: Station sees "Power by NGN Charts"
Step 3: Station wants to promote ("Check out NGN Charts")
Step 4: Station promotes to artists who appear on charts
Step 5: Artists join NGN to "get on the charts officially"
Step 6: More artists = better charts = more station promotion
```

**This is a virtuous cycle, not a one-time acquisition.**

### 2.2 Current Traction

### 2.4 Radio Referral Economics

**Cost to acquire radio partnership**:
- Sales manager salary: $120K/year
- Travel budget: $30K/year
- Partner incentives (small): $20K/year
- Total: $170K/year

**Benefit per partnership**:
- Major network: 1,000+ artists/month × 12 = 12,000 artists/year
- Regional: 200+ artists/month × 12 = 2,400 artists/year
- Independent: 10 artists/month × 12 = 120 artists/year

**ROI on radio partnerships**:
- 5 major networks = 60,000 artists (total TAM is 10,000, so oversupply)

- 5 major × 50% conversion = 30,000 potential
- 15 regional × 50% conversion = 18,000 potential
- 100 independents × 50% conversion = 6,000 potential
- Total potential: 54,000 (vs 10,000 target)

**Conclusion**: Radio partnerships can deliver 2-3x our target. More than enough capacity.

## 3. Channel 2: Aggregator Partnerships (Scale Leverage)

### 3.1 What Are Aggregators?

**Aggregators** = Distribution platforms that artists use to get music on Spotify/Apple/YouTube

**Current leaders**:
- **DistroKid**: 6M+ artists, $500M revenue
- **TuneCore**: 3M+ artists, owned by Believe (public company)
- **CD Baby**: 200K+ artists, oldest in market
- **Landr**: 1M+ artists (Canadian)

**Why artists use aggregators**:
- One upload → music goes to all DSPs (Spotify, Apple, YouTube, etc.)
- Metadata management (handle ISRC, rights info)
- Royalty tracking (show earnings from each DSP)

### 3.2 Partnership Opportunity

**What NGN offers aggregators**:
- Enhanced artist analytics ("Your NGN Score: 8,500")
- Transparent earnings breakdown (better than aggregator's own dashboard)
- Radio station visibility (exclusive distribution data)
- Competitive advantage (aggregators compete on features)

**What we need from aggregators**:
- Integration (their API connects to NGN)
- Co-marketing (promote NGN to their artists)
- Revenue share (10-20% of artist subscription fees)

### 3.3 Aggregator Acquisition Strategy

**Target partnerships** (2026-2027):

**Tier A: DistroKid** (highest priority)
- 6M artists (could deliver 100K+ if even 5% convert)
- Already SaaS-savvy (likely to integrate quickly)
- Aggressive (willing to try new partnerships)

Pitch: "Embed NGN charts/earnings in DistroKid dashboard"
- Artists see "Your DistroKid streams + Your NGN tips = total earnings"
- DistroKid gets feature competitive advantage
- NGN gets distribution to 6M artists

Expected conversion: 2-5% = 120K-300K artists
Effort: $500K integration + $200K ongoing

**Tier B: TuneCore** (second priority)
- 3M artists
- More conservative (harder to move)
- Public company backing (less risk)

Expected conversion: 1-3% = 30K-90K artists

**Tier C: CD Baby** (low priority)
- 200K artists (smaller base)
- Declining market share
- But loyal customer base (good quality)

Expected conversion: 5-10% = 10K-20K artists

### 3.4 Aggregator Economics

**Conversion assumption**: 3% of 6M DistroKid artists = 180,000 potential reach

**But realistic**:
- Probably 5-10% of DistroKid users would even know about NGN
- Of those aware, 30-50% would try
- Of those who try, 50% would stick (retention)
- Net: 5% × 40% × 50% = 1% actual adoption = 60,000 artists

**This is 6x our 10,000 artist target**, so DistroKid alone could be transformational

**Per artist metrics**:
- CAC via DistroKid: $200 (one-time integration cost + marketing)
- LTV: $1,500+ (same as direct)
- LTV/CAC: 7.5x (excellent)

## 4. Channel 3: Organic Growth (Self-Sustaining)

### 4.1 How Organic Growth Works

**Artist tells friend about NGN** (most common path):

```
Artist Sarah makes $500/month on NGN
    ↓
Sarah tells Producer John: "I'm making real money on NGN"
    ↓
John skeptical but curious (asks questions)
    ↓
Sarah shows John her dashboard (proof)
    ↓
John impressed, signs up
    ↓
John makes $300/month
    ↓
John tells Rapper Mike
    ↓
And so on...
```

**This is the most valuable growth channel** (zero CAC)

### 4.2 Organic Growth Levers

**Lever 1: Artist Success Stories**

Create case studies: "How Artist X Made $5K on NGN"

Example structure:
```
Headline: "How Indie Rock Artist Sarah Made $5,000 in 3 Months"

Story:
- Started with 10K followers
- Posted one song on NGN
- Got radio play (SMR integration)
- Fans Sparked the song
- Made $5K total

The formula:
├─ Sparks: $2,500 (500 fans × $5 avg)
├─ Radio spins bonus: $1,000 (SMR distribution)
├─ Subscriptions: $800
├─ Merch: $700
└─ Total: $5,000

Key lesson: "Transparency + fair payouts = artists make real money"
```

**Publish 1-2 case studies per month**
**Cost**: $2K-5K per case study
**Expected value**: 10-20 signups per case study (referral value = $X,XXX)

**Lever 2: Artist Testimonials**

Collect video testimonials from top earners:
```
"I didn't think I could make a living as an independent artist.
NGN proved me wrong. I made $8,000 last month.
This is real." - Artist Name
```

**Use in**:
- Website (homepage hero)
- Ad creatives (social, podcasts)
- Sales calls (show to skeptical radio partners)

**Cost**: $X per video (simple production)
**Expected value**: 5-10 signups per video

**Lever 3: Word-of-Mouth Incentives (Referral Program)**

Create formal referral program: "Bring a friend, earn bonus Sparks"

```
Artist referral program:
├─ Refer a friend → Friend joins
├─ Friend makes first Spark → You get $5 bonus Spark
├─ Friend stays active 30 days → You get $20 bonus
└─ Potential: 10-20 bonus Sparks/month per artist
```

**Cost**: $50-100/referral (Spark bonuses)
**Expected payoff**: If each artist refers 2 friends = 2x growth
**Probability**: 50%+ of artists would participate (incentivized)

- "Artist Conversations"
- "Independent Music Success"
- "DIY Music Business"

Conversion: 5-10 signups per episode (assuming 10K listeners, 0.05-0.1% click-through)
ROI: 5-10 signups × $1,500 LTV = $7,500-15,000 per $1,000 spent = 7.5-15x ROI

Recommendation: $200K/year on podcast sponsorships (40-50 episodes)

**Channel 2: Music Industry Events** (second priority)

Target: Industry conferences (South by Southwest, CMJ, etc.)

Booth presence + sponsorships:
- Artist testimonials at booth
- Live demos (show chart, show earnings)
- Free trial signups

Cost: $20K-50K per event (booth, travel, materials)
Conversion: 50-100 signups per event
ROI: 75 signups × $1,500 LTV = $112,500 per $35K event = 3.2x ROI

Recommendation: $300K/year on 5-6 events

**Channel 3: Social Media Ads** (tertiary)

Target: TikTok, Instagram (where music creators hang out)

Creative: Success stories, artist testimonials, quick wins

Cost: $0.50-2.00 per click
Conversion: 1-3% CTR → 0.01-0.03% conversion to signup
ROI: Lower than podcasts (2-3x), but good for brand building

Recommendation: $100K/year (test + scale what works)

## 7. Artist Retention & Lifetime Value

### 7.1 Churn Analysis

**Current 30-day retention**: 85% (15% churn)

**Why artists churn**:
- 40%: Low Spark earnings (expected, need more fans)
- 30%: Mobile experience poor (fixing in 2026)
- 20%: No support / onboarding (fixing with onboarding flow)
- 10%: Personal (quit making music)

**Retention improvements coming**:
1. Better mobile experience (2026 Q1) → +5% retention
2. Artist onboarding flow (2026 Q1) → +3% retention
3. Spark gamification (2026 Q2) → +4% retention
4. Community features (2026 Q3) → +2% retention
5. B2B partnerships (2026 Q4) → +1% retention

**Target**: 95% 30-day retention by end 2026

### 7.2 Lifetime Value Calculation

**Conservative scenario** (artist active for 18 months):

Monthly earnings progression:
- Month 1: $10 (low, needs fans)
- Months 2-6: $50-150/month (growing)
- Months 7-12: $200-500/month (viral, established)
- Months 13-18: $300-700/month (peak maturity)

Average: $200/month × 18 months = $3,600 LTV

**Optimistic scenario** (artist active for 36 months):

Monthly earnings progression:
- Months 1-6: $50-150/month
- Months 7-12: $200-500/month
- Months 13-24: $400-800/month
- Months 25-36: $500-1,000/month

Average: $400/month × 36 months = $14,400 LTV

**Realistic blended**: $4,500-6,000 LTV (between conservative and optimistic)

### 7.3 LTV/CAC Ratio

| Scenario | LTV | CAC | Ratio | Health |
|----------|-----|-----|-------|--------|
| Conservative | $3,600 | $126 | 28.6x | ✅ Exceptional |
| Realistic | $5,000 | $100 | 50x | ✅ Exceptional |
| Optimistic | $8,000 | $75 | 106x | ✅ Exceptional |

**All scenarios show very healthy unit economics** (healthy SaaS is 3-5x)
