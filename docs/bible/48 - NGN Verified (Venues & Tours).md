48 - NGN VERIFIED (VENUES & TOURS)

Defining the "Physical Data Node" status for industry partners.

Verified Venues: Venues using Bouncer Mode to capture fan data. In exchange, they earn a 5% Facility Fee on all Spark tips within their geofence.

Verified Tours: Routing logic driven by Affinity Heatmaps. Payouts are instant; metadata is captured at every tour stop.

The Kit: Logistics management handles the fulfillment of physical beacon hardware to all Verified locations.
