46 - INSTITUTIONAL SUCCESSION PATHS

NGN and Graylight utilize a role-to-function alignment based on long-term institutional stability.

Director of Physical Operations: Responsibility for hardware fulfillment, Aether node inventory, and logistics management.

Director of Creative Logic: Responsibility for UI/UX data visualization, chart aesthetics, and dashboard logic.

Chief of Staff: Responsibility for board governance, SIR Registry enforcement, and fiduciary accountability.

Governance Goal: All operational roles are employed by Graylight Creative (Landlord) to provide a service buffer for NGN (Tenant) activities.
